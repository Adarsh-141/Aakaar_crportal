About & Responsibilities

Aakaar’s College Representative Program is the heartbeat of our outreach. We’re on a mission to inspire college students across India to discover the exciting world of Civil Engineering. As a representative, you’ll grow your network, sharpen your skills, and gain hands-on experience you won’t find in classrooms.

Be a Face of Aakaar

Represent one of India's most prestigious technical festivals at your college.

Be a Communication link

Act as a bridge between the organizing team and your college, ensuring smooth flow of updates and information.

Be a Reporter

Collect feedback from participants and share insights with the central organizing team to improve outreach and engagement.

Be a Leader

Organize and host small-scale promotional or knowledge-sharing activities on campus.

Be a Pioneer

Collaborate with clubs, societies, and student groups to enhance participation and visibility.

Be a Brainstorm

Collaborate with brilliant minds to create impactful initiatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

You will be the face of Aakaar in your institute and the first point of contact for any student who requires information about Aakaar. As a CR, you'll bridge the gap between your college and our fest, helping students participate and engage with various opportunities.

Students from any engineering college can register. We welcome enthusiastic individuals who are passionate about civil engineering and want to make a difference in their college community.

The Program lasts until the main fest in March (approx. 5 months). During this period, you'll engage in various activities, complete tasks, and build your network while representing Aakaar.
